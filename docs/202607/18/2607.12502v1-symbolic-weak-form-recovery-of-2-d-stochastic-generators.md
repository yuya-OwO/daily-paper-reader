## 摘要
从轨迹数据恢复二维伊藤生成器存在困难，因为漂移增量具有低信噪比，双变量弱设计可能病态，且无约束的张量估计不一定是半正定的。我们研究了WG-SINDy估计器，该估计器结合了协方差形空间核、岭稳定局部多项式投影、自适应LASSO/STLSQ选择、一次样本内每分量可行的对角广义最小二乘法传递、以及带有轻微各向同性收缩的半正定投影-乔列斯基读出。发布的估计器使用数据依赖的全云平滑器和一次样本内每分量可行的对角广义最小二乘法传递；因此，我们并未声称精确的有限样本鞅消除或所报告实现具备可行GLS效率定理。我们在29个合成二维系统上评估该估计器：其中19个达到了其声明的每系统恢复合同，8个作为命名限制保留，2个仍为范围审查。在19个通过的行中，中位数中央网格漂移度量为0.204，中位数张量误差为0.0397。在六个具有有限、非退化非对角目标的系统中，中位数$a_{12}$余弦为0.997。半正定性由构造强制执行。这些结果是合成的、样本内采样区域诊断，并不建立通用或真实数据恢复。

## Abstract
Recovering two-dimensional Ito generators from trajectory data is difficult because drift increments have low signal-to-noise, bivariate weak designs can be ill-conditioned, and unconstrained tensor estimates need not be positive semidefinite. We study WG-SINDy estimator combining covariance-shaped spatial kernels, a ridge-stabilized local-polynomial projection, adaptive-LASSO/STLSQ selection, one in-sample per-component feasible diagonal GLS pass, and a PSD projection--Cholesky read-out with mild isotropic shrinkage. The released estimator uses a data-dependent full-cloud smoother and one in-sample per-component feasible diagonal GLS pass; accordingly, we do not claim exact finite-sample martingale cancellation or a feasible-GLS efficiency theorem for the reported implementation. We evaluate the estimator on 29 synthetic two-dimensional systems: 19 meet their declared per-system recovery contracts, eight are retained as named limits, and two remain scoped reviews. Across the 19 PASS rows, the median central-grid drift metric is 0.204 and the median tensor error is 0.0397. Among the six systems with a finite, non-degenerate off-diagonal target, the median $a_{12}$ cosine is 0.997. Positive-semidefinite validity is imposed by construction. These results are synthetic, in-sample sampled-region diagnostics and do not establish universal or real-data recovery.
